Another way to enjoy your banana is by making a smoothie with bananas.

Why make this banana smoothie

  • You need less than 5 minutes to make it
  • You can use fresh or frozen bananas
  • It's easy to adapt to what you have in the kitchen
  • This smoothie is perfect for breakfast or a snack
  • Kids love it!
  • You can even make the smoothie ahead of time, you can freeze it and save it for later

Ingredients

Item Unit Measurement
Banana 1 cup
Orange, peeled and quartered 1/2 cup
Greek yoghurt 1/3 cup
Water or milk(dairy or non-dairy) 1/4 cup
Honey(optional) 1-2 tbsps

Directions

Roughly chop banana and orange quarters then add to a blender. Top with yogurt and the water (or milk). Turn blender on and blend until creamy and smooth. Taste then adjust with honey if needed.

Nutrition serving:

Serving Size 1 kid-size smoothie / Calories 122 / Protein 5 g / Carbohydrate 23 g / Dietary Fiber 3 g / Total Sugars 16 g / Total Fat 2 g / Saturated Fat 1 g / Cholesterol 5 mg
